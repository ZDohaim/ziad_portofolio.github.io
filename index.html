Embracing Challenges: My Journey in Technology and Management Introduction Hello! I'm Ziad AlDohaim, a student at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering. My passion lies in the intersection of technology and management, where innovative solutions meet strategic implementation. This blog post is a reflection of my journey, aspirations, and the invaluable lessons I've learned through my BADM 261's readings and guest speakers. Inspirational Insights from Guest Speakers This semester, two guest speakers profoundly impacted my perspective and approach in both my academic and personal life. The first, Zack Quarton, shared a piece of wisdom that has since become a guiding principle for me: "Be comfortable in the uncomfortable." This advice resonated deeply, sparking a significant shift in how I approach challenges and uncertainties. Applying this mindset, I began to actively seek out challenging situations, whether it was in tackling intricate engineering projects or simply putting in extra effort in the gym. I realized that the moments I felt out of my depth were opportunities to expand my knowledge and skills. For instance, in a recent group project, I took the lead in an area where I had limited experience. Despite the initial discomfort, this decision allowed me to learn from the hands-on experience and contributed significantly to the project's success. Moreover, embracing discomfort has also improved my ability to navigate group dynamics. Working in diverse teams can be challenging, with varying opinions and work styles. However, by stepping into these situations with an open mind and a willingness to adapt, I've been able to foster more effective collaborations and gain insights from different perspectives. Conrad Chu's insights served as a perfect complement to Quarton's philosophy, further shaping my approach to learning and personal development. Chu emphasized a concept that many find counterintuitive yet crucial: embracing failures as invaluable learning experiences, especially during our student years. This perspective was a game-changer for me, significantly altering my approach to risk-taking and experimentation.This advice inspired me to adopt a more experimental mindset in my projects and studies. I started to venture beyond the safe boundaries of conventional methods and tried innovative approaches, even if they carried a risk of failure. In summary, Conrad Chu’s advice has been instrumental in shaping my approach to learning and personal growth. It has taught me to view failures not as roadblocks but as integral parts of the journey towards success. This mindset has been particularly empowering in my field of study, where innovation often involves navigating uncharted territories and learning from the outcomes, successful or otherwise. Career Aspirations and Technological Innovations My career aspiration is to become a pioneer in bridging technology with business. I envision starting a startup that not only excels in technological innovation but also makes a societal impact. A significant step towards this goal was my project to develop a device that aids the deaf through vibrations. This prototype, a fusion of engineering ingenuity and empathy, is a testament to my commitment to creating inclusive technologies. This venture is not just a business idea; it's a mission to make technology accessible and beneficial for all, especially those with disabilities. It aligns perfectly with my career goal of being a business owner at the forefront of tech innovation, where every creation is a step towards a more inclusive world. Key Learnings from the Course The course in technology and management has been a treasure trove of insights. It taught me the importance of balancing technical expertise with strategic thinking. One key takeaway is the significance of understanding consumer needs and market dynamics, which is crucial for any technology-based business. Another learning is the value of collaboration and diversity in teams. Working on various projects, I realized how diverse perspectives lead to innovative solutions. These experiences have not only enriched my knowledge but also shaped my approach to future endeavors in technology and management. Personal Reflection and Future Outlook Reflecting on my journey, I see a path defined by curiosity, resilience, and a relentless pursuit of innovation. The lessons from guest speakers, combined with my course learnings, have equipped me with a unique perspective on technology and management. Looking ahead, I am excited about the opportunities that await in the tech industry. I am determined to contribute significantly, bringing forth solutions that are not only technologically advanced but also socially responsible. My goal is to be at the helm of a venture that exemplifies innovation with a purpose. Conclusion As I continue my journey, I am confident that my experiences, backed by my education and personal values, will make a positive impression on future employers. This blog post is not just a reflection of my past but a glimpse into my aspirations. I am committed to making a difference in the world of technology and management, and I eagerly look forward to the challenges and triumphs that lie ahead.

Unleashing the Potential of AI: A Journey in Technology and Management Reflecting on the world of T&M and all the talks we've had throughout the semester, one theme stands out in my mind: the power of Artificial Intelligence, I particularly remember this most since it was the last topic we talked about, and it was the most interesting topic for me. Throughout this semester, I've had the privilege of using AI the help of ChatGPT, I believed I had a good general understanding of how it worked, but when the guest speaker was explaining things I started to get more and more interested in how it actually works, the intricacies behind it, the math and algorithms behind it. One of the most memorable experiences during this semester was listening to experts in almost every field when it comes to T&M and reading the articles, the article I remember the most is the Microsoft one that talked about the economic impact of AI.x It was interesting to learn that AI is already making a huge difference in the business world, with 71% of companies utilizing AI and achieving a remarkable average return of $3.5X for every $1 invested [Alysa Taylor, 2]. This left me pondering the endless possibilities that AI presents, not just for established businesses but also for becoming an entrepreneurs like myself.

Nowadays, AI is my top priority when thinking about my future professional objectives. I want to take an AI course to learn more about the ins and outs of AI, with the goal of eventually going back to school to get a master's degree. One potential goal I may have is to start an AI agency that helps companies—especially those who are not very tech-savvy—integrate AI into their models and strategies by providing specialized AI solutions. Businesses would be able to fully utilize AI for growth and innovation thanks to this agency's role as a liaison between technology and management. That was the instant I realized how important ChatGPT was. ChatGPT made AI accessible to anyone with an internet connection. The potential to produce text that is human-like and offer insightful information has the power to completely transform a variety of industries, including customer interaction and business procedures (I could have used it as inspiration for this writing).

To survive in today's fast-paced world where people have short attention spans, businesses must stay ahead of the curve. AI enables businesses to adapt and surpass client expectations. The availability of ChatGPT and other AI technologies allows developers to easily incorporate AI into their projects, which promotes creativity and agility. Teams are able to concentrate on high-value tasks and grow more quickly thanks to their increased agility. I agree with Microsoft's pledge to empower millions of people worldwide with AI skills in response to this challenge. It is consistent with my view that democratizing access to AI knowledge is necessary to spur innovation and close the skills divide. We can unleash AI's potential to revolutionize industries and businesses by equipping people with AI knowledge. In an era where AI is reshaping the business landscape, I am excited to be part of this transformative journey, armed with the knowledge and determination to make a difference. As I step into the world of AI, I am reminded that through the words of Zack Quarton that I have to comfortable in the uncomfortable, the only limits that exist are the ones we place on ourselves.
